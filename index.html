About Medlink Clinic

We are a young company looking to push the boundaries of excellence in healthcare consulting.

Our Commitment to Excellence

Medlink Clinic is a 100% black-owned and managed Human Consulting Company based in Pretoria, South Africa. Our business activities are focused on Human Resources Consulting, Medico Legal services, Psychometric Assessments, and Career Guidance, and are not restricted in nature.

We are a young company committed to pushing the boundaries of excellence and delivering exceptional service to our clients. As a small-medium enterprise, our clients experience personalized attention and dedicated support from our team.

Why We Exist

Improve Quality: We ensure all experts have undertaken Medico-legal Training, with all new experts completing the Bond Solon Medical Expert Training Courses.

Ease Communication: Communicating between Solicitors and Medical Experts can be challenging. Medlink Clinic provides a single point of contact to streamline interaction and ensure clarity.

Reduce Cost: As government legislation tightens and linkage increases, there is a growing need to reduce litigation costs. We remove traditional intermediary fees to make services more cost-effective.

Reduce Complexity: We simplify the process of instructing an expert, making it repeatable and efficient, supported by our bespoke case management system.
